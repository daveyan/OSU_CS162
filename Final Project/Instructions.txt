Instructions:

The Struggle is a text base game that challenges the user to collect the required items before the time runs out.

At different times in the game, you will be prompted with an action.

[A]ction	:Action

In this situation, enter the character 'A' or 'a' to use the action.

Most of the movement will be done with the WASD keys, but note that there will be special actions bound to similar letters.
